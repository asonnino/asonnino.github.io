DECODE

Giving people ownership of their personal data.

DECODE is a European Commission funded project exploring and piloting new technologies that give people more control over how they store, manage and use personal data generated online. We will test the technology we develop in two pilot sites and will explore the social benefits of widespread open data commons. Read more.
